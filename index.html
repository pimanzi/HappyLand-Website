OUR MENTORS

Our mentors are grouped into two specialized categories: resume review and career advice. In the resume review category, mentors offer tailored feedback to optimize your resume for job applications. In the career advice category, mentors provide personalized guidance to navigate career decisions and advance professionally. With dedicated support in these key areas, our mentors are committed to empowering you on your journey to success.

ABOUT US

This group of five students came together with a shared vision: to facilitate connections between students and mentors at the University of HappyLand. Their collective goal is to bridge the gap between students seeking guidance and experienced mentors willing to offer support. Through their initiative, they aim to create a vibrant community where mentorship thrives, empowering students to achieve their academic and professional aspirations with confidence and clarity.
